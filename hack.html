Hack for Purpose

IIT Delhi Hackathon

Important Dates

  • Deadline: 22nd March 2024
  • Preliminary Submission Deadline: 23rd March 2024
  • Final Round: 29th March 2024

Guidelines

  1. Participants l form teams consisting of 1-4 individuals.
  2. The hackathon consists of 3 segments, each focusing on a distinct area of innovation.
  3. Teams must select one segment to participate in and work on the corresponding problem statement provided for that segment.
  4. Participants can work on 2 or more segments as well.
  5. Teams are required to submit a rough idea of their project in PDF/PPT format.
  6. Each submission should be at most 5 slides (PPT) or 3 pages (pdf).
  7. If one team is working on multiple segments they should provide a PDF for each segment.
  8. The top 20 teams from each segment will be selected to advance to the next stage.
  9. Selected teams will compete offline at IIT-D on 29th March.
  10. Awards will be provided to the best three ideas of all three segments combined:
